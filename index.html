📚 Complete Cheat Sheet

📊 Performance Score Guide

PageSpeed Score (0-100):
🟢 90-100: Excellent - Lightning fast
🟡 80-89: Good - Decent speed
🟠 70-79: Needs Improvement - Getting slow
🔴 0-69: Poor - Unacceptably slow
YSlow Score (0-100):
🟢 90-100: Excellent - Follows all best practices
🟡 80-89: Good - Minor optimizations needed
🟠 70-79: Needs Improvement - Several areas to fix
🔴 0-69: Poor - Major performance issues
Fully Loaded Time:
🟢 0-2s: Excellent - Instant loading
🟡 2-4s: Good - Acceptable speed
🟠 4-6s: Slow - Users might leave
🔴 6s+: Very Slow - High bounce rate

🍪 Cookie Colors Explained

GREEN - Session Cookie:
Good! Temporary, expires when browser closes. Helps website function properly.
YELLOW - Tracking Cookie:
Warning! Tracks user behavior across visits (Google Analytics, Hotjar, etc.).
RED - Third-party Cookie:
Problem! Other companies watching your visitors (Facebook Pixel, Google Ads, LinkedIn).
BLUE - Preference Cookie:
Info! Stores user settings (language, theme, shopping cart, login remember).

🔍 Cookie Details

🟡 Tracking Cookies:
• Google Analytics (_ga)
• Facebook Pixel
• Hotjar
• Tracks: Pages visited, time spent, clicks
🔴 Third-party Cookies:
• Facebook Pixel
• Google Ads
• LinkedIn Insight
• Other companies know your visitors
🔵 Preference Cookies:
• Language selection
• Theme/color scheme
• Shopping cart items
• Login remember me
• Makes site convenient for returning users

📋 YSlow Score Details

What YSlow Measures:
• Use CDN for images
• Compress components with gzip
• Put CSS at top
• Put JavaScript at bottom
• Minimize HTTP requests
Client Explanation:
"YSlow is like a report card for how well your website follows performance best practices. While PageSpeed measures actual loading speed, YSlow checks if your site is built efficiently."

💬 Client Talking Points

Cookie Explanations:
• "Green cookies help your website work properly"
• "Yellow cookies help understand how people use your site"
• "Red cookies help with advertising but respect privacy"
• "Blue cookies remember visitor preferences"
Performance Explanations:
• "PageSpeed measures how fast your site loads"
• "YSlow measures how well your site follows best practices"

💡 Quick Compliance Tips:

• CCPA/CPRA: Applies to businesses with CA residents, $25M+ revenue, or processes 100k+ consumers
• GDPR: Applies to EU citizen data regardless of business location
• State Laws: Vary by revenue thresholds and data processing volumes
• Cookie Consent: Required for tracking cookies in regulated states

🔒 CCPA Requirements

• Right to know what data is collected
• Right to delete personal data
• Right to opt-out of data sales
• Right to non-discrimination
• "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" link
• Data processing agreements required

🌍 GDPR Requirements

• Lawful basis for processing
• Data subject access rights
• Right to be forgotten
• Data portability
• Privacy by design
• Data protection officer (if needed)
• 72-hour breach notification

📋 Website Compliance

• Privacy policy with specific disclosures
• Cookie consent banner
• Data collection transparency
• Opt-out mechanisms
• Data security measures
• Vendor management
• Record keeping
